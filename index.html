Location

Brooklyn is a suburb of Cape Town, Western Cape. It lies to the east of the Atlantic Ocean and Paarden Eiland.

Our development is conveniently situated. It's just a 10-minute drive to Cape Town CBD, making it ideal for those who work or study in the city. If shopping is your passion, you'll be pleased to know that Canal Walk is only 5 minutes away.

Additionally, Paarden Eiland is just 2 minutes from our development, providing a tranquil escape from the city bustle.
